FTC 7303 Maryland Tech Invitational Robot

This Robot was made by my team, FTC 7303 RoboAvatars, to compete in the First Tech Challenge Competition, Freight Frenzy. We made it for the Maryland Tech Invitational at Johns Hopkins University on 6/22/22. It has an extending intake. The intake itself is coaxially powered, allowing it to be packaged compactly. The outtake has angled linear slides and a short arm.This robot propelled us to be on the third-seeded alliance, which we are extremely happy about, considering the Maryland Tech Invitational has the best 50 teams worldwide.
