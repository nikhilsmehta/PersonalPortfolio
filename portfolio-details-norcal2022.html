FTC 7303 Norcal Regionals Robot

This Robot was made by my team, FTC 7303 RoboAvatars, to compete in the First Tech Challenge Competition, Freight Frenzy. We made it for Norcal Regionals, which took place on 3/14/22. This robot is by far the most complex robot I have helped build. It features an extending intake, a 13-inch long arm, mounted on linear slides that extend 5 feet out of the robot, which is all mounted on a 360 degree turret. I learned many lessons from this robot, with the main one being that complex robots are not always better than consistent simple robots. Here is a youtube video showing a 255 point match by this robot. This entire robot was made in CAD before being made in real life.
