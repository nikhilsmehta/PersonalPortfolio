About

I am a 10th grader at Mission San Jose High School in Fremont, California. I enjoy CADing, building robots, making cool Arduino projects, and 3D printing with my Ender 3 Pro. I am on a competitive robotics team, FTC team 7303 RoboAvatars, where I mainly work on CADing, machining, and building the robot.

Experience

I have experience in many STEM fields including Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, CAD, and Machining.

  • Fluent in 4 CAD softwares: Onshape, Fusion 360, Autodesk Inventor, and Blender.
  • 7 years of competitive robotics experience (First Lego League and First Tech Challenge)
  • Familiar with Arduino and Raspberry Pi systems.
  • 3 years of experience with modifying and using 3D printers (Creality Ender 3 Pro and Prusa i3 MK3S
  • Experienced in designing for good accesibility, manufacturability, and tolerances.
  • Taught classes on CAD, programming, and introduction to competitive robotics.
